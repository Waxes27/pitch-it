Business Model

Revenue model is driven by strategic collaboration between our 3 core products:+ Trustd app: our free app that grows brand awareness and provides a wide lens of threat intelligence to power our revenue-generating solutions;+ Trustd MTD: our b2b revenue-generating mobile threat defence solution, sold through channel partners;+ Trustd Plus: our b2c revenue-generating premium app subscription.

Exit Strategy

Similar to recent MTD acquisitions (e.g. Jamf's acquisition of Wandera for $400m), our exit strategy is a 4 year trade sale with a plan to merge with or be acquired by a Unified Endpoint Management (UEM), Unified Security Management (USM) or Extended Detection & Response (XDR) solution provider
The Market

Market Research

The rise in mobile-targeted threats, like phishing and malware, is leading an increasing number of businesses to adopt MTD solutions. As a result, the MTD market has experienced bullish growth to $350m in 2020, at a 32.3% CAGR between 2018 and 2020. Gartner predict that by 2025, 50% of organisations will have mobile security solutions in place. Whilst adoption of MTD solutions is growing, existing solutions are overly complex, deliver ineffective security and invade employee privacy - particularly when it comes to BYOD.
